## 1. Introduction

Gira++ ("the App") is an independent, community-developed companion application for the Gira bike-sharing service in Lisbon, Portugal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

**Important:** Gira++ is NOT affiliated with EMEL, Câmara Municipal de Lisboa, or the official Gira service. This is an independent third-party application.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Location Data
- **Precise Location:** Used to show your position on the map and find nearby bike stations
- **Background Location (optional):** If enabled, used to track your trip route while the device is locked
- **Purpose:** Essential for the core bike-sharing functionality

### 2.2 Authentication Credentials
- **Email and Password:** Entered by you to log into your existing Gira/EMEL account
- **How we handle it:** Your credentials are sent directly to EMEL's authentication servers. We do NOT store, log, or have access to your credentials. They are transmitted securely and never stored on our servers.

### 2.3 Usage Data
- **Trip History:** Retrieved from EMEL's servers (not stored by us)
- **App Preferences:** Stored locally on your device only

---

## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ We do NOT collect personal identification information
- ❌ We do NOT track your behavior for advertising purposes
- ❌ We do NOT share any data with third parties
- ❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- ❌ We do NOT store your login credentials

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Location | Show on map, find stations | Device only (not transmitted to us) |
| Credentials | Authenticate with EMEL | Passed through, never stored |
| Preferences | Remember your settings | Device only |

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- All app preferences are stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers
- Communication with EMEL servers uses secure HTTPS encryption
- We do not operate any backend servers that store user data

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following services:

- **EMEL/Gira API:** For authentication and bike station data (governed by EMEL's privacy policy)
- **OpenStreetMap/MapLibre:** For map display (no personal data shared)

---

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR)

As an EU resident, you have the right to:

- **Access:** Request what data we have about you (Answer: none stored by us)
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data
- **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw Consent:** Stop using the App at any time

**To exercise any rights related to your Gira/EMEL account, please contact EMEL directly.**

**To clear local app data:** Go to your device Settings → Apps → Gira++ → Clear Data

---
